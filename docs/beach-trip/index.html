Where it lands

The honest read

Every contender is nonstop, swimmable and family-friendly — and saves ~$3,500–5,600 vs Montage (live Montage Nov ~$9,040).

Cheapest: Corazón Cabo, Nov — ~$3,500. Same swimmable Cabo bay, tiny price.

Closest to Montage vibe: Conrad Punta de Mita, March — ~$4,550 (and PV dodges the spring-break flight spike).

New flight insight: for Cancún & Cabo, November beats March once you count airfare — spring break jacks those fares up ~$500/person. Only Puerto Vallarta stays cheap in March.

Is New Year's week (Dec 26–31) better? No — it's the priciest of all four. Nonstops roughly double ($1,642–1,813/pp), Montage & Conrad are already sold out, and rooms add a holiday premium. Even the cheapest, Corazón, jumps to ~$7,040 (vs ~$3,500 in November).

Like Conrad? Look at Puerto Vallarta. W Punta de Mita is its value twin (~$4,390 in March, same calm bay + design vibe), Marriott PV is the cheapest and closest to the airport (~$3,600, 10-min transfer), and Four Seasons is the dream if budget opens up. PV also dodges the spring-break flight spike, so March stays cheap here.
